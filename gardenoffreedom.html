Sitting still in your evermoving routine
On your little balcony, gazing at the world
Or on your way to work on the busy street
Focused on your own problems

A traffic light is red, you stop
It’s cold, you tuck your soft scarf on your coat
Green, you go
Walking in a crowd
How lonely it is
No connections, no strings
Only your own thoughts

You daydream, about a different place
A place for everyone to come together
To discuss everyday issues that affect you
To hear and be heard,
To decide together about the future of your city
A place that includes all voices and opinions
Lively and buzzing like a garden

That place is not that far away
Make connections, speak to each other
Come closer, sit at the table
Let’s see and let’s talk it out
Only together do we have the power to make our futures better
Only together we can build our arch
Only together we are a polis.
